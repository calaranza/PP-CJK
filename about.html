About Lumine

History

 Lumine was founded in 2026 by young talented designers who believed that clothing should reflect on who we are as a person displaying confidence, simplicity, and individuality. Starting from small beginnings of pop-up booths to having its own store locations, Lumine quickly gained attention for its clean and minimalistic looking designs and comfortable fabric. Inspired by the word "Luminous" which means to light up, the brand was created to help people express themselves and stand out in their own unique way. As Lumine continues to expand and grow day by day, it still stays true to its original vision of its designers combining style, comfort, and self-expression in which everyone can wear.
