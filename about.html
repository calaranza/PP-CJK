About Lumine

Lumine was founded in 2025 by young talented designers who believed that clothing should reflect on who we are as a person displaying confidence, simplicity, and individuality. Starting from small beginnings of pop-up booths to having its own store locations, Lumine quickly gained attention for its clean and minimalistic looking designs and comfortable fabric. Inspired by the word "Luminous" which means to light up, the brand was created to help people express themselves and stand out in their own unique way. As Lumine continues to expand and grow day by day, it still stays true to its original vision of its designers combining style, comfort, and self-expression in which everyone can wear.

Frequently Asked Questions

When and what time is Lumine open?
Our store hours open daily from Monday to Sunday at 9:00am to 10:00pm.
Where can I find Lumine stores?
Our shops are currently located at three mall locations: SM Mall of Asia, Uptown Mall Bonifacio Global City, and Greenbelt Makati
What products does Lumine sell?
We sell top quality products from t-shirts, polos, hoodies, jackets, dresses, and blouses.
What does Lumine mean?
The name of our brand came from the word Luminous, which means "to shine or glow". Our clothes allow customers to exude confidence and define their self-expression with vibrance.
Does Lumine offer nationwide delivery?
Yes, we deliver our products nationwide, which are subject to shipping fees. Shipment within Mega Manila may take 2-4 business days, while shipment outside of Mega Manila may take 5-7 business days.
Can I return an item?
Yes, items can be returned as long as it is within 7 days from the day it was bought or delivered and in good condition.
